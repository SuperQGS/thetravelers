Text-Bullet

Text-Bullet is the backbone of a Travelers game, but running it alone won’t do much. To get the desired mechanics & functionality, it will need plugins.

The most important plugin currently is “the-travelers-plus”. It re-implements the mechanics from thetravelers.online, other than the puzzle locations. You can think of Text-Bullet as the Source Engine, and the-travelers-plus as Half-Life 2.

After that, there is an assortment of community plugins to further alter behavior. If you don’t find what you want, try making your own plugin and sharing it with us!

You can download Text-Bullet here and the-travelers-plus here.
