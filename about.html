So, what's The Travelers?

The Travelers Online is a text-based MMO created by torogadude. It features a post apocalyptic scavenger hunt that took a year for the community to solve.

We are that community. After solving the puzzle, we decided we weren’t done with this niche little browser game. With torogadude’s blessing, we reverse engineered it. We made it modular, modifiable, and open source.

We turned it into this, The Travelers Live. A community-made spiritual successor. Not just one game, but a network of games. A network where anyone can take this idea we feel has so much potential, and experiment with it in any direction they desire.
