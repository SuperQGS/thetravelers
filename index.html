A Player Led Survival MMO centered around traveling. The Travelers Live is a platform and network built for players by players.

Explore apocalyptic wastelands, loot abandoned cities, uncover dark secrets, or do something entirely different.

Browse the server list to find your ideal experience, or tinker around to create something completely new.

The Travelers Live is open source, and anyone is free to host whatever experience they like.
